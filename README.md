# 🚀 Never wait in line again.

### AI-powered virtual queue management for hospitals, banks, government offices, and beyond.

**wAItless** is an AI-powered virtual queue management platform that creates a **Virtual Human Placeholder (VHP)** to securely hold your place in line. Users receive a virtual token, track their queue in real time, and return only when their turn is near—turning wasted waiting time into productive time.

---

# 📌 The Problem

Every day, millions of people lose valuable time waiting in queues just to keep their place. Traditional queue systems demand physical presence, leading to overcrowding, uncertainty, and reduced productivity.

> **The problem isn't the queue—it's the waiting.**

---

# 💡 Our Solution

**wAItless** introduces the **Virtual Human Placeholder (VHP)**—a secure digital presence that waits on behalf of the user.

Instead of standing in line, users can:

- 📱 Scan a QR code
- 🎫 Receive a virtual queue token
- 🚶 Leave the waiting area
- 📍 Track their position in real time
- 🤖 Receive AI-powered ETA predictions
- 🔔 Get notified when it's time to return

### 🚀 Wait Virtually. Arrive Only When Needed.

# 🧠 AI That Predicts, Not Just Displays

Unlike traditional queue systems that simply display the current token number, **wAItless** predicts the **actual expected service time** using:

- Queue length
- Average service duration
- Historical waiting patterns
- Staff availability
- Emergency interruptions
- Real-time delays

### Example

```text
15 People Ahead

Average Consultation : 8 min

Doctor Delay : 20 min

Estimated Call Time

12:45 PM ±10 min
```

---

# 📍 Smart Queue Radius (Geo-Fencing)

To ensure fairness, users can move freely within a predefined radius (e.g. **500 m**).

- ✅ Inside Radius → Queue remains active
- ⚠️ Outside Radius → Warning notification
- ❌ Doesn't return on time → Queue position may be reassigned

This provides flexibility without compromising fairness.
